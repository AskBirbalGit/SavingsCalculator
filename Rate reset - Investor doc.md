**Why we win**

In India, the problem is not access to credit anymore — it's the inability to optimize it. Every rate cut creates a new cohort of borrowers paying more than they should. No one has built the infrastructure to fix that at scale. We are going to build it. 

**1\. Reset-first, switch-second — our AI decides which path wins before the borrower even asks**

Every other platform defaults to balance transfer because that's where the commission lives. Our AI evaluates the borrower's profile, lender history, outstanding principal, and live benchmark spreads to determine in real time whether a reset or a BT delivers better savings — and then executes that path. The decision is dynamic, personalised, and happens in minutes. No human bias. No commission-driven routing. Customer first. 

**2\. Proprietary negotiation engine — not a comparison table**

We don't show borrowers a rate table and ask them to choose. Our AI has mapped lender reset thresholds — which banks move, for which borrower profiles, at what outstanding balance, with what language — and generates a negotiation strategy specific to each case. This is not something you can reverse-engineer from public data. It only exists inside the negotiation. Best decision made for the customer

**3\. Personalised savings engine — not an estimate**

Our AI ingests the borrower's current rate, loan age, outstanding principal, credit score, and live MCLR/EBLR spreads to compute the exact rupee savings from reset versus BT — including switching costs, processing fees, and tenure impact. The output is not "you could save up to ₹X." It is "your savings are ₹X,XXX per month from Day 1 if your lender resets by this date, or ₹X,XXX if you move to this specific lender." End outcome made happen for customer. 

**4\. Two revenue streams — AI optimises for the higher-margin outcome**

If the existing lender resets → we charge a reset facilitation fee. If BT is executed → lender acquisition fee from the new lender. Most platforms only earn on BT. We earn on both — and our AI is explicitly designed to attempt the reset path first, because the reset outcome carries near-zero CAC, zero credit risk, and 100% gross margin. The BT path averages 1% of loan value. Again what suits customer better is our choice too.
